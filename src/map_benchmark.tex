 At the time this book is written (2018) it has been twenty five years since the last machine came out of \NeXT Redwood City factory in 1993. It has become next to impossible to see a functioning machine for sale.\\
 \par
 It was important to get one for this book, in order to reproduce the working condition of the time and witness the full game pipeline in motion. Even though passionate and dedicated programmers have managed to produce a gorgeous emulator called "Previous", the number would have not been accurate.\\
 \par
  Thanks to Rob Blessin, owner and founder of Black Hole, Inc, I was able to acquire the NextStation Color Turbo Model\cw{N1200} \cw{\#ABC0053943}. The SCSCI hard-drive was making weird noise so it was replaced by a SD card SCSI2SD with similar access time. The MegaDisplay colors had faded out so a NEC MultiSync 1980SX able to sync on green came out to the rescue.\\
